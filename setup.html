Get Your Own Starship (Duplicating the App)

Music Galaxy is an open-source template. By clicking the magic "Use this template" button on GitHub, you create your completely free, private copy of the entire application. It runs forever without a backend server.

2

Turn on the Engines (GitHub Actions)

Because you now own your copy, you need to tell GitHub to automatically update your website every time you add a new song. We do this by turning on "GitHub Actions".

In your new repository on GitHub:

  • Click the Settings tab at the top.
  • Click Pages in the left sidebar.
  • Under the Build and deployment section, change the "Source" dropdown from 'Deploy from a branch' to GitHub Actions.
3

Gather Your Fuel (Playlists)

The engine runs strictly on YouTube URLs (either full playlists or individual song links). It scrapes the metadata behind the scenes to map your clusters.

Wait, I Use Spotify or Apple Music!

No worries! You can't paste Spotify links directly, but you can use an incredible free tool like TuneMyMusic or Soundiiz to clone your Spotify playlists to a free YouTube account in just a single click. Once copied over, grab those new YouTube playlist links and you're good to go!
