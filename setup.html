MUSIC GALAXY

Build your personalized universe, completely free. No coding required.

1 Get Your Copy

We use GitHub to host the magic for free. Click 'Use this template' to duplicate the engine into your own private account.

CLONE TEMPLATE

2 Turn on the Engine

To automatically map the stars whenever you add a song, you need to flip a powerful switch called 'GitHub Actions'.

  • Go to the Settings tab of your new repository.
  • Click Pages in the left sidebar.
  • Under 'Build and deployment', change 'Source' to GitHub Actions.

3 Gather Fuel

The engine runs on YouTube Links (Playlists or Individual Songs). You can add as many as you want in step 4.

[!] WARNING: SPOTIFY USERS

Spotify links don't work directly here! But don't panic.

Use a free tool like TuneMyMusic to instantly clone your Spotify playlists into a YouTube account. Then grab those shiny new YouTube links!

4 Map The Stars

Once your new site is live, simply visit it and click the ⚙️ EDIT PLAYLISTS button in the top right.

Paste your YouTube links into the text box, hit Commit, and in exactly 3 minutes... your galaxy is born!
